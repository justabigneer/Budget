About Budgetly

Our Mission

Budgetly is designed to help individuals take control of their personal finances through simple, intuitive budget management tools. We believe that financial wellness should be accessible to everyone.

What We Offer

  • Budget Planning: Set monthly income and savings goals
  • Expense Categorization: Organize expenses with default and custom categories
  • Visual Analytics: Understand spending patterns with charts and graphs
  • Goal Tracking: Monitor progress toward your savings objectives
  • Secure & Private: Your financial data is protected and private

Technology

Budgetly is built using modern web technologies including Django, Bootstrap, and Chart.js to provide a fast, responsive, and user-friendly experience across all devices.
